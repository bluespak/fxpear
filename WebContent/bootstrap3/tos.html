The lowdown

We (the folks at FX PEAR.com) offer a service that matches people (our users) who want to trade currency with eachother. While our service's main goal is to achieve a mutually beneficial currency exchange outcome for the users we connect, it is not within the scope of our service to provide or imply financial advice (e.g. when to buy or sell a currency) to our users. We advise our users to apply their own common sense when buying, selling or trading currency, and if in doubt to seek the guidance of a professional financial advisor.
